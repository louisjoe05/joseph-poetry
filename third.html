ROSES

Roses, what a beauty in the nature,
Embellishing the world with wonderful colors.
Attracting the attention of the admirer,
and nourishing the hearts of the lovers.

Open up your ears and listen to my voice, says the gardener
Roses in the garden are beautiful souls on the earth.
Listen close to the wisdom of the wise for great is the creator;
The roses hold a tremendous wealth.

Sit beside the garden and learn a lesson,
Notice how the roses struggle for their lives and glory in evry season;
The bloom is hard but the rose is not selfish, A bee talk to another bee:"friend rose bloomed again, we can not perish".

Emblem of beauty,grace and happiness are the roses.
"Respect i don't carry a thorn in vain", says the rose,
"I live for the sake of others my existence is not vain;
But wide is my heart, a beautiful soul of beautiful colors i will remain"
